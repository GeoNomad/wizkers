Notes about device log: the "Memory Statistics" above gives you an indication of how many records your Onyx can hold, and how full it currently is. It also tells you how many days/hours of logging are remaining in the instrument's flash.

"Download Log" transfers the current log to the computer. The app can detect whether this is a new log, or if we should append to an existing log (same starting timestamp as an existing log on the computer). "Download Log" is disabled is the log is empty.
