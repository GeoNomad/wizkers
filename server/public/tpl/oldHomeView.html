Welcome!

This is a simple Onyx log viewer, nothing fancy.

Please to to the "settings" menu and setup the serial port of your Onyx.

You can then connect to your device - wait until the "Connect to Onyx" button turns green, and display the contents of the logs. On the "Diagnostics" screen, you can check the version of the firmware, run a display test on the device, and synchronize its internal clock to your computer.
